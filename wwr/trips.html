Our Most Enjoyed Trips

Enjoy a relaxing time kayaking on one of our lakes or smooth flowing river sections. Some rivers may have class I rapids. All equipment provided. For river trips, return transportation is also provided. Kayaking trips can be booked for 2, 4, or 6 hours. Bring sunscreen, water, snacks and a wide brimmed hat is recommended. Lunch can be made available as part of the 4 or 6 hour trips.

Our half-day excursions are on areas of the river with class I to class III rapids. Go Pro cameras are available for rental. All other equipment provided and return transportation back to starting location is also provided. Optional lunch can be purchased. This trip is good to explore if you want to make river running a regular part of your outdoor experience. A mid-trip checkpoint is available for those who may want to end their trip early. Minimum age of 10 years old.

Our Full-Day Rapids are for the adventuresome individual/group. Plan to get wet. Do not bring any possesions on this excursion that you can not afford to lose in the river. Three optional checkpoints available are available if needed. These boats need a minimum crew of 8 so if your party is smaller than that you will be combined with other individuals/groups. Lunch provided. Minimum age is 12 years old. All needed equipment provided.

Extreme-Rapids are great for the thrill seakers. Some of our biggest and most dangerous rapids are included in these trips. Individuals must be at least 16 years old. These trips can be scheduled for 1-3 days which would include camping and hiking excursions on parts of day 2 and/or 3. All necessary equipment provided. Meals can be purchased for the length of your trip.
